---
title: "Getting Started With DT FairBid"
---

DT FairBid is a mediation platform that gives developers and publishers control over which ad networks to integrate, how to prioritize them (if using a waterfall approach), and how to optimize ad placements to maximize revenue. DT FairBid supports integration with an array of leading ad networks.

To get started with DT FairBid:

## Set Up Your App in the DT Console

As a DT Publisher, add your app and placements to the DT ecosystem via the [DTConsole](https://console.fyber.com/). For more information about how to set up your app and placements for DT FairBid, see[SettingUpDTFairBid](https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/en-us/articles/360009974718-Setting-Up-DT-FairBid). For more information about how to become a DT Publisher, see [SigningUp for DTConsole](https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/en-us/articles/17532003308828-Signing-Up-for-DT-Console).

## Integrate the DT FairBid SDK

The DT FairBid SDK is platform-specific for Android, iOS, and Unity and allows you to offer your placements across all mediated networks. For more information about how to integrate the DT FairBid SDK, see[SDKIntegration](/hc/en-us/articles/16884209848092).
