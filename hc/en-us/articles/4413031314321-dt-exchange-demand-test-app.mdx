The Digital Turbine Exchange Test App is a self-service test tool that allows you to test your integration and creatives on DT Exchange. This tool allows you to validate components such as the close button, video skip button, countdown timer, video skipability standards, and close button delays.

To validate ads on the DT Demand Test App, you must have access to the DT self-service web dashboard and the [DT Demand Web Tool](https://forest.fyber.com/demand#/). Once you have the credentials, configure the Web Tool and the Demand Test App:

* [Step 1: Download the Demand Test App](#h_01JQNHSQB0VCHQ0GTWS6AQDXFR).
* [Step 2: Upload Ads using the Creative Tester](#h_01JQNJCEWPYDC28C3WAEKMRJES).
* [Step 3: Test the Ad on your device](#h_01JQNHT2WPEG2ZKVF1WGANMXTY).

## Ad Testing Methods

The Demand Test App supports two testing methods:

* Ad Lifecycle: Tests end-to-end live integrations. DT Exchange sends a bid request to your servers and guarantees the winning of your bid response. The ad you chose to serve is displayed in the test app for you to validate.
* Creative Tester: Tests a mock bid response that you can upload in advance using the web tool. You can test the uploaded ad in the test app.

#### Note

You can access only the Creative Tester Tool if your integration is not yet live. Once your OpenRTB integration goes live, you can use the Ad Lifecycle tool.

## Step 1: Download the Demand Test App

To download the Demand Test App:

1. Log in to the [Demand Web Tool](https://forest.fyber.com/demand) with your user credentials.
2. Click **App Links**.\
   The iOS and Android links appear.

<Frame caption="">
  <img src="https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/article_attachments/4419870805137" />
</Frame>

3. Download and install the required platform on your device.

## Step 2: Upload Ads Using the Creative Tester

#### Note

To test an ad using Ad Lifecycle only, proceed to [Step 3](#h_01JQNHT2WPEG2ZKVF1WGANMXTY).

To upload Ads and test them on the Demand Test App:

1. In the [Demand Web Tool](https://forest.fyber.com/demand), click **CREATE** to add a new creative.
2. In the description field, add a description for your creative.
3. To add openRTB parameters, click **+** (plus) and add DT-supported parameters described in [DT Exchange OpenRTB 2.5 Specs](/hc/en-us/articles/360010835237) in a key-value format.
4. In the **markup** field, paste the HTML or VAST ad markup, similar to the content of the `adm` field in an Open RTB response to your ad markup.
5. Click **SAVE**.\
   A **QR code** appears on the Web Tool.

## Step 3: Test the Ad on Your Device

To test ads:

1. In the [Demand Web Tool](https://forest.fyber.com/demand), access the QR code for the test ad:

* * To test an**Ad Lifecycle**, click **Ad Lifecycle**.**\
**The QR Code Appears.
  * To test a **Creative**, click **Creative Tester** and then click **Show** for the required creative.\
    The QR Code Appears.

2. To test the app on your device, open the **DSP Test App** and follow the instructions in the appropriate tab below.

Android iOS

To test ads on an Android device:

1. Tap the QR code icon.

<Frame caption="">
  <img src="https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/article_attachments/4544559105169" />
</Frame> 

2. Direct the camera toward the QR code.
3. Select an ad format from the dropdown menu in the screen's center.
4. Tap the ad you want to test and then tap **Load**.\
   The rendered ad appears.
5. To test **Interstitial** and **Rewarded** ads, tap **Load** and then **Show**.\
   The rendered ad appears.

#### Tip

To use Chrome Developer Tools to inspect and remove devices, see [DevTools Devices](https://developer.chrome.com/docs/devtools/settings/devices).

To test ads on an iOS device:

1. Tap the **+** icon on the top left corner and then tap **New Ad Unit**.
2. Tap the **QR code** icon.

<Frame caption="">
  <img src="https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/article_attachments/4528184546833" />
</Frame> 

3. Direct the camera toward the QR code.
4. Select the ad format you want to test from the Ad Format dropdown list.

<Frame caption="">
  <img src="https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/article_attachments/4528542261265" />
</Frame> 

5. Tap **Save Ad Unit** at the bottom of the screen.

#### Note

All added units appear in **Saved Ads,** located on the Home Page, which allows you to test ads without scanning the QR code each time.

6. Tap the ad you want to test and then tap **Load**.\
   The rendered ad appears.
7. To test **Interstitial** and **Rewarded** ad formats, tap **Load** and then **Show**.\
   The rendered ad appears.
