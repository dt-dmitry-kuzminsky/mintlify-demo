## Step 1. Adding Digital Turbine as a Partner Network

1. Click on **Ads→Partner Management**. Select *Digital Turbine(Unified)*.

<Frame caption="Ads_PM_DTUnified_20240515.png">
  <img src="https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/article_attachments/14000693201948" />
</Frame>

2. Click on the **POSTBACK CONFIG**tab.

<Frame caption="Ads_PM_DTUnified_Postback_20240515.png">
  <img src="https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/article_attachments/14000946649116" />
</Frame>

3. Check the boxes under** ALL EVENTS** to send unattributed postbacks to Digital Turbine for optimization purposes. This also helps us exclude users from targeting.
4. Click on the **ATTRIBUTION WINDOWS** tab.

<Frame caption="Ads_PM_DTUnified_AttWindow_20240515.png">
  <img src="https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/article_attachments/14001224975260" />
</Frame>

5. Ensure that the **Use ad partner attribution settings box** is checked and match the window settings above.

#### Note

This is the standard setting for the lookback window. If you have any questions about how your specific campaign should be set up, please consult your Digital Turbine representative.

6. Click **Save**.
7. Click **Create Digital Turbine(Unified) Link**.

<Frame caption="Ads_PM_DTUnified_AttWindow_Create_20240515.png">
  <img src="https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/article_attachments/14001588460316" />
</Frame>

8. Under **App Only**, select **App Install or Engagement** and click **Continue**.

<Frame caption="Ads_PM_DTUnified_CreateAdLink_20240515.png">
  <img src="https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/article_attachments/14001588463260" />
</Frame>

9. Enter a name for the link in the **Name your link** field.

<Frame caption="Ads_Links_Define_20240515.png">
  <img src="https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/article_attachments/14001588464796" />
</Frame>

10. Verify the **Link Type** and **Link Domain**.
11. Verify that the **Which Ad Partner is this for?** is *Digital Turbine(Unified)*.
12. Select an OS type from the **Select OS type** dropdown.
13. Click **Configure Options**.
14. On the **DEEP LINKING** tab, add any extra key-value pairs that are required.

<Frame caption="Ads_Links_Config_DeepLink_20240515.png">
  <img src="https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/article_attachments/14002092323612" />
</Frame>

15. On the **REDIRECTS** tab, validate the **iOS**, **Android**, and **Desktop** redirects.

<Frame caption="Ads_Links_Config_Redirects_2024-05-15_13-51-01.png">
  <img src="https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/article_attachments/14001886880156" />
</Frame>

16. (Optional) On the **ANALYTICS TAGS** tab, enter **Channel**, **Campaign**, or **Tag** values.

<Frame caption="Ads_Links_Config_Analytics_2024-05-15_13-51-01.png">
  <img src="https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/article_attachments/14001886882204" />
</Frame>

17. Click **Save**.
18. Copy the **Click Tracking Link** and the **Impression Tracking Link**, and send them to your Digital Turbine Representative.

<Frame caption="Ads_Links_ValidShare_20240515.png">
  <img src="https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/article_attachments/14002085092892" />
</Frame>

## Step 2. Enabling Agency Permissions

1. Click on **Account Settings**.

<Frame caption="Acct_Settings_20240516.png">
  <img src="https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/article_attachments/14018227337244" />
</Frame>

2. On the **AGENCIES** tab, click on **Add New Agency**.

<Frame caption="Acct_Settings_Agencies_20240516.png">
  <img src="https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/article_attachments/14018227339676" />
</Frame>

3. Search for and select *Digital Turbine(Unified)* as the **Agency Name**.

<Frame caption="Acct_Settings_AddAgency_20240516.png">
  <img src="https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/article_attachments/14018227342876" />
</Frame>

4. Verify that **Access Level** is set to *Custom*.
5. Match the **Permissions** for **View** and **Edit** to the image above.

#### Note

This will give Digital Turbine the ability to view campaigns settings and basic reporting for troubleshooting purposes. If you have any questions on these settings, please reach out to your Digital Turbine representative.

6. Match the **Data Filters** toggles to the image above to ensure that only *Digital Turbine(Unified)* data is shown.
7. Click **Invite**.

## Step 3. Setting Up Cost Tracking

At this time, Branch cannot send cost data directly to Digital Turbine.However, a direct integration with our [Reporting API](https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/en-us/articles/11288748483612) is available. Please contact your Digital Turbine representative if you want to know more about this option.
