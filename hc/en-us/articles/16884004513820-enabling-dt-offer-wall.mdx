---
title: "Enabling DT Offer Wall"
---

DT Offer Wall is a monetization solution available from Digital Turbine that provides an engaging, user-friendly ad format that allows your app users to complete offers in exchange for rewards. It is a great addition to your app if you're already using DT FairBid for ad mediation. And since both DT FairBid and DT Offer Wall are part of the same SDK, you are one step away from boosting user engagement and increasing your revenue potential. See[DT Offer Wall product page](https://www.digitalturbine.com/dt-offer-wall) to learn more.
