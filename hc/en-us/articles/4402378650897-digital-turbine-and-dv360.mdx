Digital Turbine integrates with [Google Display and Video 360](https://support.google.com/displayvideo?sjid=3371801904629721729-EU#topic=9059505) (DV360) bidder, to support programmatic auctions. This integration allows you to access your Digital Turbine Exchange inventory directly through DV360, when you render ads on the Google SDK.

## Integrating Digital Turbine Exchange with Google DV360

To integrate DT Exchange with Google DV 360, do the following:

1. Log in to your Google DV 360 [account](https://marketingplatform.google.com/about/display-video-360/).
2. From the left pane, go to **Partner Settings→Basic Details**.
3. In the **Exchanges** area, click the **Edit** icon, and selectthe **Digital Turbine** checkbox.
4. Click **Save**.\
   DT Exchange is now integrated with your DV360 account.

#### Note

* App mediation is separate from the Digital Turbine's Exchange. Contact your DT representative for questions regarding app mediation.
* Admin accounts apply this setup in real time. Admin and Standard users can edit exchanges. For more information, see [Manage users in Display & Video 360](https://support.google.com/displayvideo/answer/2723011?hl=en-GB\&sjid=14823043161705942917-NC).
* Digital Turbine appears as a direct exchange partner when you set up individual campaigns.

## Key Features

The following features describe some advantages when you activateDT's inventory within DV360.

* Scale campaigns: Access 100% SDK direct inventory at full scale, which includes 25 billion auctions or 130 unique users from the premium mobile app gaming inventory.
* More Control: Provides greater control over the target and impressions available, which eliminates the need for additional filtering for your key audience.
* Better Quality: The integration renders the latest updates from DT's and Google's SDKs. It allows you to target for viewability, brand safety, and app attribution through self-reported signals. This helps you to validate supply from the Google publisher community and improve performance.
