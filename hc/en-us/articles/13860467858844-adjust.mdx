## Step 3. Contacting Adjust

Two more things that need to be taken care of are allowlisting Digital Turbine and enabling Direct Attribution.

Why is this important?

## Allowlisting

Adjust must first allowlist Digital Turbine for the tracker token. This removes Digital Turbine from being false-positively flagged by the distribution outlier filter, one of many fraud filters that Adjust has in place. This is due to the nature of our ad unit.

## Direct Attribution

Adjust must add your tracker token to enable Direct Attribution tracking as well. Enabling Direct (or Pre-Install) Attribution gives Digital Turbine longer lookback window options, deterministic matching without a device ID, and attribution priority for Digital Turbine above other ad engagements (impressions and clicks). Credit goes to Digital Turbine because the app is installed directly on device as opposed to redirecting to the Store. Direct Attribution is only applicable for apps installed through Digital Turbine’s Ignite software.

We have prepared an e-mail template that you should use: copy and paste it or, alternatively, click [Send Email](mailto:integration@adjust.com?cc=Add_Your_DT_Growth_Account_Manager@digitalturbine.com\&subject=Digital%20Turbine%20Campaign%20Settings\&body=Hello,%0D%0A%0D%0APlease%20apply%20the%20below%20settings%20to%20the%20tracker%20token%20%5BINSERT%20TRACKER%20TOKEN%20HERE%5D%3A%0D%0A%0D%0A1.%20Allowlist%20the%20above%20token%20for%20fraud%20algorithms.%0D%0A2.%20Prioritize%20Preinstall%20Attribution%20on%20the%20above%20token%20with%20a%20365%20day%20lookback%20window.%0D%0A%0D%0AThank%20you%2C) to open an (almost) ready-made draft in you default e-mail client.

#### Important

Make sure to include your tracker token, which you can look up in the [tracking links you created](#h_01HXPEAX9DDTDRGGQ8AZ8KBGK6). The tracker token is the underlined part of the URL before the *?* mark:

* Click URL

  `https://app.adjust.com/3kkxrw?campaign=[CAMPAIGN_NAME]&additional_parameters…`

* Impression URL

  `https://view.adjust.com/impression/3kkxrw?campaign=CAMPAIGN_NAME]&additional_parameters…`

## Step 5. Setting Up Cost Tracking

Cost tracking relies on Adjust Cost API. Follow the steps below to enable the Cost API.

1. In the Adjust dashboard menu, click **DataWorks→Connections** and click on the **+** icon to add a new connection.\
   The **New connection** dialog opens.

<Frame caption="">
  <img src="https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/article_attachments/16982185128092" />
</Frame>

2. In the **Partner** drop-down, start typing *Digital*and pick *Digital Turbine Growth*.
3. In the **Services** drop-down, pick *Ad Spend*.
4. In the **Client ID** and **Client secret** fields, enter the API access credentials. These are provided by your Digital Turbine representative.
5. Click **Connect**.

## Step 6. Setting Up Reporting

Adjust offers two Cost APIs for DT campaigns: the Cost API and the New DT Unified Cost API. For DT Unified campaigns, you need to explicitly switch your reporting to the new API. Complete the following steps to do so:

1. In the Adjust dashboard menu, click **Datascape→Reports** and either open an existing report or add a new report by clicking on the **+** icon.
2. In the report filters area at the top, click **Data configuration**.

<Frame caption="">
  <img src="https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/article_attachments/16982185130652" />
</Frame>

3. Scroll down or start typing *Digital *to locateand enable the **Digital Turbine ad spend version** filter and click **Apply**.

<Frame caption="">
  <img src="https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/article_attachments/16982175549212" />
</Frame>

4. The **Digital Turbine ad spend **filter appears in the filters area at the top. The filter is set to *Advertiser Reports API* by default.

<Frame caption="">
  <img src="https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/article_attachments/16982175551388" />
</Frame>

5. Click on the **Digital Turbine ad spend version** filter, set it to *Growth Reporting API (new)* and click **Apply**.
