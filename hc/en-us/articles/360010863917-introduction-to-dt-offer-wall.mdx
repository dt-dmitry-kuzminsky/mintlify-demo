---
title: "Introduction To DT Offer Wall"
---

DT OfferWall provides a means to enhance user retention while increasing revenue. By presenting your users with a selection of offers from DT Advertiser partners, your users can fulfill the offer requirements in exchange for virtual in-app currency. For more information about DT Offer Wall, see [DT Offer Wall product page.](https://digitalturbine.com/dt-offer-wall)

To get started with OfferWall:

1. Add your app and placements to the DT ecosystem via the [DTConsole](https://console.fyber.com/) as described in [SettingUp DTOfferWall](/hc/en-us/articles/4404313469201-Setting-Up-DT-Offer-Wall).
2. Integrate the DTOfferWall SDK as described in [SDKIntegration](/hc/en-us/articles/16668688781596).
3. Implement a rewarding strategy as described in [RewardingYourUsers](/hc/en-us/articles/360010865437).
4. Test your OfferWall implementation as described in [TestingYour OfferWallImplementation](https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/en-us/articles/18589296749724-Testing-Your-Offer-Wall-Implementation).
5. Ensure that your integrated app is approved in the app stores.
6. Go live with your implementation as described in [Activate Your App](https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/en-us/articles/4404313469201-Setting-Up-DT-Offer-Wall#GoLive).
