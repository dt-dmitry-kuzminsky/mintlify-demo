---
title: "Sample App"
---

Use the DT sample app as a reference during your DT FairBid implementation to troubleshoot integration issues. The sample app allows you to view and test the following ad formats:

* Banners
* Interstitials
* Rewarded Videos
* MRECs

The sample app is available on the following platforms:

Android iOS Unity

See the[FairBid sample app for Android](https://github.com/fyber-engineering/fairbid-sample-app-android) onGitHub.

See the[FairBid sample app for iOS](https://github.com/fyber-engineering/fairbid-sample-app-ios) onGitHub.

See the[FairBid sample app for Unity](https://github.com/fyber-engineering/fairbid-sample-app-unity) onGitHub.
