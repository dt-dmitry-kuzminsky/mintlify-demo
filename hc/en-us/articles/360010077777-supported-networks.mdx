Mediated Ad Network partners work together with DT to offer you a wider range of demand for your ad unit inventory.

To integrate Mediated Ad Networks, use the following interactive table:

#### Tip

For quick access to the iOS info.plist generator and updater tools, select the iOS Platform tab in the table below, and refer to Step 3.

Step 1: Selecting Mediated Ad Networks

1. Click one of the following tabs to select the required platform:

   * Android
   * iOS
   * Unity

2. From the **Configuration** column, select the required mediated ad network.\
   You can select multiple ad networks as needed.

<Note>
#### Note

Every DT FairBid SDK includes the latest version of DT Exchange, enabled by default. You do not need to check it.
