The **Dashboard Metrics** feature allows you to track campaign performance with real-time data updates at Product, Campaign, Country Group, and App levels. You can configure the metrics displayed on the **ACP Edge dashboard** to evaluate the performance of your campaign.

## Configuring Dashboard Metrics

To select metrics:

1. Select a **Product** or **Campaign** on the [ACP Edge](https://acp-edge.fyber.com/) Dashboard.
2. Click the **Metric Picker**, and select the metrics you want to appear on the dashboard.\
   The selected metrics appear on the dashboard.

<Frame caption="mceclip1.png">
  <img src="https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/article_attachments/6341260749981" />
</Frame>

3. (Optional) To adjust the time frame, click one of the **Date** fields at the top-right corner of the dashboard. Then, select the time frame to any range between the current day and the past 90 days.

<Frame caption="mceclip2.png">
  <img src="https://developer.digitalturbine.com/hc/article_attachments/6341262656541" />
</Frame>

#### Note

The **Date Picker** is accessible at the Product, Campaign, Country Group, and App levels. All data is displayed in UTC and is updated with an average four-hour delay for the current day. Metrics are based on the Billing View for consistency and accuracy.
